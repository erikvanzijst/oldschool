Overview papers
I added a few overview papers on Evolutionary Computation to the downloadables. These provide additional material to my course.
  • T. Bäck's overview gives a glossary of terms, a detailed treatment of the EC dialetcs (GA, ES, EP, GP), and some theory download postscript.
  • A.E. Eiben's introduction handles the simple GA and ES as examples to illustrate the main aspects of EC and reviews some EC application areas download postscript.
Note 1: Since these papers are not yet published their distribution is limited - only for private use and not citeable.
Note 2: If and when my slides and the papers differ, the slides are to be followed for the exam.
