This is the WWW page for the course on Computer Graphics (400106) given Fall 2004 at Vrije Universiteit. The course consists of a lecture and some programming assignments; both will be communicated in English. As people learn best when they "do it themselves", the emphasis of the course is on the practical (programming) work; this will contribute to 2/3 of the final mark. The programming will be done using the OpenGL graphics standard and the C programming language.

The course in a nutshell

lecture: wednesdays, 11:00-12:45, S.111 (on the ICWall tiled display)
weeks 37-43, 45-50
lecturer: dr. Thilo Kielmann (office P4.14)
assignment work: to be done at the Windows machines of the CS department
organized by: drs. Tom van der Schaaf, drs. Mathijs den Burger
credit points:6 ECTS credits
grading: programming assignments (2/3) + written exam (1/3)
Both parts have to be passed separately to get the credit points.
partial results: In case only one of the two parts (either programming or exam) has been passed, this result can be re-used in the next year(s).
No credit points will be awarded for partial results.
Codes are 4001068 (programming assignments) and 4001069 (exam).
book: E. Angel, Interactive Computer Graphics with OpenGL, third Edition,
Addison Wesley, 2003
available from STORM and from the VU Boekhandel
(additional material to the book)
prerequisites:programming knowledge, preferably with C

OK, your better bring some pens and your student ID...


  1. Since 2003, the exam is "closed book". However, this does not imply that you need to memorize nasty details which would better be looked up in "real life". You will have to be prepared by understanding the concepts that will have been taught in the course, ideally by doing your programming exercises well. For example, you will need to know that matrices for certain affine transformations have their typical shapes, but you won't need to know where in a rotation matrix we have a -sin and where a cos.
  2. Don't worry, we do not expect miracles from you. You can download the exam from January 2004 as an example.
